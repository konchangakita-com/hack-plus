## Alert Thresholds

| KPI | Alert Condition | Action |
|-----|-----------------|--------|
| Daily PV | <100 | Review distribution strategy |
| Bounce Rate | >70% | Improve content quality |
| Publishing Frequency | <3/week | Review content pipeline |
| Subscriber Growth | 0 for 2 months | Review pricing/value prop |

---

## Data Sources

- **Traffic**: Google Analytics
- **Revenue**: Stripe Dashboard (via Engine)
- **Content**: CMS Analytics
- **SEO**: Google Search Console
- **Social**: Platform native analytics

---

## Review Cadence

- **Daily**: PV, UU, Articles published
- **Weekly**: Traffic trends, Top content, Social growth
- **Monthly**: Full KPI dashboard, Revenue, Content ROI
- **Quarterly**: Strategic KPI review with hq

---

## Content Performance Framework

### Success Criteria per Article
- PV > 500 in first week
- Avg. Time on Page > 3 min
- Bounce Rate < 60%
- Social Shares > 10

### Top Performer Analysis
- Which topics perform best?
- Which formats (tutorial, opinion, news) work?
- What time/day to publish?
- Which distribution channels are most effective?

---

## Notes

KPIは「学習ツール」でもある。これらの数字を通じて、グローバルメディア企業が何を重視し、どうコンテンツと収益を最適化するかを体得する。
